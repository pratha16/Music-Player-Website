Music has always been a means of entertaining people even from the earliest ages of the civilization. Historically it was
produced by musicians and only available during life concerts.
The technological evolution made it possible to save the music on vinyl plates, later electromagnetic charged stripes, CDs until the technology brought us to saving tracks digitally.
When dealing with a huge collection of tracks, people encounter management problems they did not have before. So they have to develop new ways of using the music collection for their entertainment.
Playlists are a good approach for saving successions of tracks that one likes.
The most dominant problem of existing playlist generation mechanisms is, however, their lack of flexibility: new tracks are not automatically added, they dont adapt to the users current mood etc.
